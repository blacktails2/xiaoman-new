About

Looking for amazing things in life

Happy living makes a better life.

Delicious tea can establish a solid foundation for life, and we can provide a comfortable space for you to look back on your life and listen to your heart.

We provide thousands of Chinese and Taiwanese teas. We hope to spread the rich aroma of tea for everyone in the world.

The aroma arises when the hot water reaches the teacup. It can help you relaxed and allows us to pay attention to the busyness of our life.
Simply looking at the tea set is enough to calm your mind.
The earthenware tea set is fragile, please touch it gently. Immersing yourself in Chadou, all tenderness and goodwill you provide will be brought back to you.
When you relax and enjoy the world of five senses, your vision will be clearer. As the way progresses, beautiful flowers will be seen, and you will be able to identify the taste that would otherwise be difficult to detect. When you open mind and start to feel with your heart, your life will change.
The sound of boiling water and pouring tea into the teacup will heal us.
Change creates fun, and fun brings energy to our life. Just increase your awareness, look at the beauty in life and keep the energy of change.
It will be the beginning of your happy life.
